README.txt — personal.log

I'm Emilia Beeke, a game developer who believes games are more than just fun, and can be used to convey messages and bring people together. My first experience with coding started when I was just 11 years old, making simple projects in Scratch. I later moved on to trying out Python and Unity.


Today I build indie games in small teams, together with artists and other developers. My dream and goal is to create a game that gains a community that supports each other, others, and most of all: enjoys my game to the fullest. I've experience this 'safe haven' myself in multiple games I've played (take Stardew Valley for example). Because of this experience, I want to create a similar space for others.
